More About Me

I'm Gligor Iliev, a curious and motivated beginner in web development, born and raised in the peaceful village of Pirava. Life in a small village has taught me to value patience, simplicity, and the beauty of learning things slowly but surely.

Even though I'm still at the beginning of this journey, I’ve already discovered how exciting it feels to create something with your own hands — or in this case, with code. My first steps were through HTML and CSS, and from there I started to explore tools like Webflow, WordPress, and Figma to expand my creativity and understanding of web design.

Outside of the computer screen, I enjoy gaming, football, cycling, and watching movies — activities that keep me balanced and inspired. I believe every passion adds a little something to how I approach design and problem solving.

This portfolio is a reflection of my first real attempt at building something on my own. It's not perfect, but it's honest — and every line of code I write gets me closer to who I want to become: a developer who never stops learning, growing, and dreaming.
